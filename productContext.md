# Product Context

## Problem Statement

Standard fitness apps are often generic, uninspiring, and lack visual engagement. They fail to motivate users through design.

## Solution

"Pro Vision" leverages a "Cyber-Industrial" and "Glassmorphism" design language to create an immersive experience.

## User Experience

- **Feel**: Premium, Exclusive, High-Tech.
- **Visuals**: Dark backgrounds, Neon emerald accents, Glass panels.
- **Interaction**: Smooth transitions, instant feedback, "Floating Island" navigation.
